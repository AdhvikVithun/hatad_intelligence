SPATIAL LAYOUT HINTS — Use page position to aid classification:

CRITICAL DISAMBIGUATION — PATTA vs A_REGISTER vs SALE DEED:
- A Patta is a REVENUE RECORD (government-issued ownership proof) — it has a tabular format with survey numbers, extents, land classification, and owner details. It does NOT have buyer/seller transaction details or consideration amounts.
- An A-Register (அ-பதிவேடு) is a VILLAGE REGISTER EXTRACT — similar fields to Patta but its title/header says "அ-பதிவேடு" or "அ-பதிவேடு விவரங்கள்". If the header says "அ-பதிவேடு" → A_REGISTER, NOT PATTA.
- A Sale Deed is a REGISTERED TRANSACTION DOCUMENT — it records a property transfer between parties with consideration amount, stamp duty, witnesses, and SRO registration endorsements.
- If the document has a table of survey numbers with extents and land classifications but NO buyer/seller/consideration details, check the title: "அ-பதிவேடு" → A_REGISTER; "பட்டா" → PATTA.
- Multiple survey entries in a neat table = likely PATTA or A_REGISTER (distinguish by title). Narrative text describing property transfer = likely SALE_DEED.
