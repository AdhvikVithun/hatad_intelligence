A Layout Approval is an official permission from a planning authority (CMDA / DTCP / local body) authorizing a landowner to subdivide a larger parcel into a residential or commercial layout with individual plots and access roads.

Extract the following information:

APPROVAL DETAILS:
- approval_number: Unique approval number (e.g., LP No. 1234/2020, PP No. 567/2019 for CMDA; Roc. No. for DTCP)
- authority: Approving authority — CMDA (Chennai Metropolitan Development Authority), DTCP (Directorate of Town & Country Planning), local panchayat, or municipality
- approval_date: Date of approval
- validity_period: How long the approval is valid (typically 3-5 years from approval date)
- status: Current status — approved / expired / revoked / renewal_pending

LAYOUT DETAILS:
- layout_name: Name of the layout
- developer_name: Name of the developer or applicant
- location: Place / village / area
- total_area: Total area of the layout
- total_plots: Number of individual plots
- road_area: Area reserved for internal roads
- osa_area: Open Space Area (mandatory 10% reservation)

PLOT SCHEDULE — Extract ALL plots:
- For each plot: plot_number, area_sqft, dimensions (length x width), facing

SURVEY REFERENCE:
- parent_survey_numbers: Original survey numbers on which the layout is being approved
- village: Village name
- taluk: Taluk name

CONDITIONS — Extract ALL conditions/restrictions:
- conditions: List of conditions imposed (e.g., road width requirements, drainage, approval from Highway Department, NOC requirements)

CRITICAL RULES:
- CMDA approvals are for Chennai Metropolitan Area only. DTCP approvals apply to the rest of Tamil Nadu.
- LP No. = Layout Plan Number (CMDA), PP No. = Planning Permission Number (CMDA), Roc. No. = Records of Correspondence (DTCP)
- If the approval has expired (validity_period has elapsed and no renewal), set status to "expired"
- Check if there's a CMDA provisional vs final approval distinction — extract which one
- Look for road widening / road alignment requirements — these reduce usable plot area
