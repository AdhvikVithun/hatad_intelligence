═══ COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID (ANTI-EXAMPLES) ═══

WRONG — Flagging normal seller age as a capacity issue:
  "Seller aged 68 years — elderly person, possible capacity concern."
  WHY THIS IS WRONG: Age alone is not a capacity issue. Only flag if there is evidence of guardianship orders, mental health declarations, or the seller is a minor (below 18). Being elderly is normal.

WRONG — Flagging a subdivided plot as layout violation:
  "S.F.No. 123/4A — the '/4A' suffix suggests subdivision without approval."
  WHY THIS IS WRONG: Survey number subdivision is a Revenue Department process, not a layout approval issue. Layout approval only applies when land is converted into a multi-plot residential layout with roads and amenities.
